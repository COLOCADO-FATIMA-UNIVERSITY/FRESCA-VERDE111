About Fresca Verde

Fresca Verde is your one-stop destination for fresh, organic, and locally sourced grocery products. Built by passionate students, the platform aims to revolutionize the way you shop healthy by combining sustainable practices with modern technology.

Whether you're looking for farm-fresh vegetables, dairy alternatives, or organic pantry staples, we bring it all directly to your doorstep — with a commitment to quality, transparency, and convenience.

Join our journey to build a greener and healthier future. Support local. Shop consciously. Live sustainably with Fresca Verde.

We never sell your data to third parties. Your information is stored securely and used solely to enhance your experience with us. You may request to review, modify, or delete your personal data at any time.

Cookies Policy

Our website uses cookies to improve performance and provide personalized features. Cookies are small text files stored on your device that help us remember your preferences and enhance your experience.

By using our site, you agree to our use of cookies. You can disable cookies in your browser settings, although this may affect site functionality.

We use both session and persistent cookies for analytics, login preferences, and to keep your shopping cart active.

Shipping & Delivery

At Fresca Verde, we prioritize timely and secure delivery of your organic groceries. We partner with trusted logistics providers to ensure your items arrive fresh and in excellent condition.

Delivery Areas: We currently serve Metro Manila and nearby provinces. More areas will be added soon as we expand our reach.

Delivery Time: Orders placed before 4 PM are typically processed the same day and delivered within 1–3 business days, depending on your location and item availability.

Shipping Fees: A flat delivery fee applies for standard shipping. Free delivery is available for orders exceeding a certain minimum amount — promos and updates are announced on our homepage and newsletter.

Order Tracking: After checkout, you will receive an order number to track your delivery status through our "Track Your Order" feature.

Failed Deliveries: If a delivery attempt fails, our team will contact you to reschedule. Additional delivery charges may apply after the second attempt.

We are committed to ensuring your groceries arrive safely, sustainably, and on time. Thank you for choosing Fresca Verde!
